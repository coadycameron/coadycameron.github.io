Host City - Fredericton, New Brunswick

Fredericton is a perfect mix of big city feel and small town charm. Nestled along the Saint John River in the south central part of the province, Fredericton has been included twice in international rankings of the Top 7 Smart Communities in the world. The event will be held at the Fredericton Convention Centre, the province's prime meeting place located in the heart of the capital. The convention centre boasts high-speed internet and is adjacent to multiple hotels, local shops, and restaurants.
